 Privacy Policy for "دمُك حياة" App

Introduction  
The privacy of our users is a top priority for the "دمُك حياة" app. This privacy policy aims to clarify how we collect, use, and share the information we gather when creating an account in the app.

---

Information We Collect  
When creating an account in the "دمُك حياة" app, we collect the following information from users:  
- Name  
- Phone Number  
- Address  
- Residential Address  
- Blood Type  
- Age  
- Weight  
- Gender  

---

How We Use Information  
We use the information we collect for the following purposes:  
1. Connecting Blood Donors: We use the information to facilitate communication between blood donors and individuals in need of blood.  
2. Improving Our Services: We use the information to better understand user needs and enhance the app.  
3. Communicating with Users: We may contact users regarding app updates or important information related to blood donation.

---

Sharing Information  
We may share the information we collect with other users of the app who are in need of blood donors. We are committed to protecting the privacy of shared information and ensuring it is used only for blood donation purposes.

---

Information Security  
We take appropriate security measures to protect the personal information we collect. While we strive to safeguard information, we cannot guarantee complete security of data over the internet.

---

Changes to the Privacy Policy  
We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Users will be notified of any significant changes through in-app notifications or via email.
